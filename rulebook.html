Overview

Corellian Spike is a variation of Sabacc. It is a gambling cardgame for two to eight players. It is comparable to the Earth games "Poker" and "Black Jack." { This page is a work in progress. There should be a whole lot of rules here. }

Other Hands

All other hands are ranked in this order (positive beats negative when judging highest numbers):

    • Sabacc (adds up to zero)

    • Sabacc with the most cards

    • Sabacc with the highest positive number

    • Sabacc with the highest positive number

    • Nulrhek (closest to zero)

    • Positive Nulrhek

    • Positive Nulrhek with the most cards

    • Positive Nulrhek with the highest positive sum

    • Positive Nulrhek with the highest single positive card

If a tie still exists, tied players draw a single card. The player whose card is closest to zero wins the pot.

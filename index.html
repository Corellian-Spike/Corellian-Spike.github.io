Corellian Spike

Corellian Spike is a cardgame developed by Lucasfilm Ltd. for the film Solo: A Star Wars Story.
It is a variation of Sabacc.

We have re-created the rules of the game played in the movie, based on various fan and canonical resources, as well as real-world playtesting.
